Experiment Concept Iteration

For my project development, I created a design comp of my final Every Picture concept as my experiment project wasn't directly representative of what I would do for the project. For my comp, I overlayed my piano sheet music on top of an image I had of my piano and am showing how the video of me playing the song should pop up when one of the sheet music pages are hovered. I want to be able to have the active page come in front of others when hovered as well as the video auto play. I'm also considering zooming into the page when it is hovered but that will be an extra interaction to play with if I have time. For now, I will be focusing on getting the positioning of all the pages right and creating the video pop-up above each page when they are hovered.
